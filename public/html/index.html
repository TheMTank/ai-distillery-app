What we intend to do

Automatically model and distil knowledge within AI.
In other words, summarise the arxiv/research firehose.
Map, categorise, quantify, qualify, filter, search, browse, reduce, digest, compress, summarise and model all knowledge within ML/DL/RL/AI/DS/CV/NLP/CS/Stats.
Always for the community.

Other related goals:

  • Have an automatically updated web page and dashboard for showing any of the relevant insights and visualisations we want for the world to see.
  • Information Retrieval: Help researchers find what they are looking for and help the community
  • Show what is currently trending within AI. Predict future trends.
  • Show how a specific field or the general field of AI evolves over time.
